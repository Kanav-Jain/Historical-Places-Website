YOUR TRAVEL GUIDE TO GREECE

Temple of Olympian Zeus

One of the best places to visit in Athens, the temple of Olympian Zeus has been named after God Zeus and is also dedicated to him. It is known to be one of the largest temples in the ancient world and possesses an interesting history. It’s a picturesque site with a propylon gate and corinthian columns.

Academy of Athens

Academy of Athens is also a name in the list of historical places in Greece that bestow knowledge about the country. It is the oldest research center in Greece. One of the well-celebrated historical places to visit in Greece, the Academy of Athens is considered to be the heir of Plato’s Academy. It honors the country’s researchers, scientists, and humanists.

Ancient Agora of Athens

Ancient Agora of Athens was an ancient place where people usually gathered and met for assemblies, conferences and also to buy things sometimes. Owing to its name, which means ‘marketplace’ in Greek, it witnessed huge gatherings.

Mount Lycabettus

Known as the highest point in Athens, Mount Lycabettus provides stunning views of the surroundings and is one of the most beautiful places to visit in Athens. Although quite a challenge to climb up the hill possessing a circular path, the walk up is a beautiful experience. To enjoy the most bewitching views, it is advisable to visit Mount Lycabettus during the sunset and it will leave you mesmerised.

Parthenon

Built in reverence of Goddess Athena, Parthenon is an ancient temple making for one of the best places to see in Athens. Providing glimpses into the Greek history, this temple is a paragon of robustness and has retained its charm over the years. Although destroyed once, Parthenon has its historical significance intact with the lifelike sculptures alluring the tourists from all over the world.
